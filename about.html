About Me

Hello! I'm Fred Otieno, a dedicated Full-Stack Web Developer based in Nairobi, Kenya. With a deep passion for writing clean, efficient, and robust code, I specialize in crafting modern, high-performance web applications that bridge visual aesthetics with rock-solid backend infrastructure.

My technical journey is built on structured education and hands-on client deployments. I specialize in the MERN Stack (MongoDB, Express.js, React.js, Node.js), TypeScript, and responsive CSS architectures. From building luxury, interactive lodge booking portals to dense cyberpunk-themed analytics dashboards, I treat coding as a craft that demands constant refinement and detail.

As a developer, my philosophy is simple: user experience is paramount. I strive to build software that not only operates flawlessly under the hood but feels organic, responsive, and natural to the end user. I actively integrate third-party APIs (like Lipa na M-Pesa STK push checkouts) and specialize in adapting sites for fast load speeds, clean typography, and search engine optimizations.
